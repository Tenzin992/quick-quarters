Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order amount?

The minimum order amount is $20 in quarters per delivery.

How far do you deliver?

We deliver within a 25-mile radius in the San Francisco Bay Area. Additional fees may apply for distances beyond this range.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments through Venmo and Zelle. Payment details will be provided after order confirmation.

Yes, additional orders can be placed with a small per-order fee.
